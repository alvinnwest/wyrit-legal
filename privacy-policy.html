Wȳr-It Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Wȳr-It ("the App", "we", "us") is a digital wardrobe and styling app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account information. Email address, name, username, and profile photo. If you sign in with Google, Facebook, or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from that provider.
  • Wardrobe content. Photos of clothing items you upload, and the details you add about them (brand, size, category, care info, notes).
  • Generated content. Processed versions of your photos (for example background-removed and AI-rendered garment images, and virtual try-on renders) created to power App features.
  • Social and marketplace activity. Content you choose to share with friends or list for sale, messages you send in the App, and live show participation.
  • Device information. Push notification tokens and basic device data needed to run the App.

How we use your information

  • To provide the App's features: cataloguing your wardrobe, AI garment processing and try-on, outfit building, styling suggestions, calendar planning, social sharing, live shows, and marketplace listings.
  • To send notifications you enable (for example outfit reminders and social activity).
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the App.

We do not sell your personal information.

How your information is shared

  • Other users: only the content you explicitly share (public listings, live shows, items or outfits you make visible to friends).
  • Service providers: we use Supabase (data storage and authentication), LiveKit (live video), Expo (push notifications), and AI image-processing services to operate App features. These providers process data on our behalf.
  • Sign-in providers: Google, Facebook, and Apple, when you choose to sign in with them.

Data retention

Your content is retained while your account is active. When you delete an item, its photos are removed from storage. When you delete your account, your personal data and wardrobe content are deleted.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual items at any time in the App. To delete your account and all associated data, see our Data Deletion Instructions.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date on this page.
